## About this Specialization

The institute is taking its pioneering steps towards Cyber Security by starting this Interdisciplinary course for Cyber Security and Cyber Defense of Critical Infrastructures and Geo-Data sharing. The course brings both theoretical as well as use cases on how cyber defense and awareness technology can be effectively applied to better understand and manage against cyber threats.

## Course Objective

The course mission is to research, educate, train and also spawn startups in Remote sensing data and geo- informatics and create technological safeguards to protect critical infrastructure.
